About Forfend

Forfend is an IoT based company focused on developing people and asset tracking solutions. Our vision is to unlock the power of IoT technology in solving day-to-day and real world challenges that can be used by masses. Today, it is estimated that there are over 40 billion devices and over a trillion sensors in the world. While more and more organizations are catching the IoT bug, what is more important is its meaningful application. Our vision and focus is to apply the IoT technology in the area of asset and people tracking, solve day-to-day challenges and bring solutions that are affordable and available for masses.

Our first set of products digiFence and digiTrack help track people and assets, inside premises and outside premises.

digiFence enables tracking and creating a secured environment for people and assets within and without premise – be it schools, apartments, corporate, hospitals or hotels.

digiTrack helps tracking your field force or assets in geographically distributed area – be it your bill collection agent for Utilities, delivery boys for e-commerce companies or a sales force of a pharmaceutical company. It has unique ability to track both people and assets, without any dependency on mobile
