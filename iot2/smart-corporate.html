SMARTCORPORATE

OFFICE THAT IS AGILE, ALIVE & AWARE

Smart Corporate

A smart corporate is more agile and collaborative. Not just that – it is lean, efficient and operates with highest utilization – whether it is people or assets.

In last couple of decades, the unprecedented growth in businesses, such as IT / ITeS / Ecommerce made corporates setup large offices across India to tap the talent and grow even faster. Today, corporates have ended up with office spaces with lower efficiencies and high cost of maintenance. Most importantly the offices are not able to cater to the ‘Gen-Y’ and the agile culture without huge investments in redoing the place.

Forfend’s IoT based SmartCorporate solution helps you to effortlessly address these challenges and offers endless possibilities to achieve the following:

  • Improve operational efficiencies
  • Increase productivity
  • Reduce costs
  • Enhance safety

Some Scenarios

Smart Seating

Quick allocation of seats for a new project is important to start earning the revenues. However, this process is tedious and takes manual intervention to allocate the right seats. In the process there is a lot of revenue leakage owing to lack of billing. Smart Seating allows you to allocate the seats with a click of a button. No manual work no manual visits to the location. This saves significant costs and most importantly enables you to start filling the spaces and bill the client right away.

Smart Engagement

We have people coming in from various parts of the cities and unfortunately, their seats are hard wired and will have to go across the city to work. The Smart Engagement enables you to book a seat of your choice and at an office of your choice and start working. This improves the employee motivation as she can work from any place of her choice depending on the need. Also, it improves the collaboration between employees and makes the culture even more engaging.

Smart Visitor Management

Visitor management is a tedious process in any large corporate with endless security checks. Smart Visitor Management helps you to register a visitor once and track her movements in the premise. There is no need to unnecessarily spend the bandwidth of the security escort. Also, any movement of the visitor from the set path will alert the entire security for immediate action. This makes the entire process simple, yet very secure.

Smart Security

In case of an emergency or security situation a Smart office responds fast and can help save lives or injuries.
