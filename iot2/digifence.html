digiFence

DigiFence is our flagship product for in premise people and asset tracking. The product comes with a secure wireless network, end points id card/tag and mobile application to track the asset or people. The controller module and access points form the network. The easy to deploy network tracks people and assets at easily accessible areas and in all remote areas like basements, across multiple floors. The digiFence works well at premises where the traditional GPS based products have limitations.

Key features

  • Covers entire building or the office complex, including basements and other secluded interior areas of building.
  • Precisely tracks people / assets at individual floor or room level
  • Interacts with sensor points like door access control, wrist devices, or power switches
  • Sets up tress pass zones passed on the time/space constraints per asset
  • Sends alerts to mobiles, tablets or PCs on the internet or intranet
  • Generates various tracking and security reports as per customer needs
