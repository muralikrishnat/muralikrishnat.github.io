digiTrack

When you have a field force covering geographically distributed area spreading to thousands of square kilometers, it is quite a daunting task to keep track of them. You may want to track your field force to ensure they are following the planned route map, or communicate with them on a real time basis for effective sales conversations. digiTrack helps you track the location of the field force. It also helps manager assign the routes, track the field force personnel on a real time and communicate with them on need basis. All this without any smart phone.

How does it work?

We will first setup a GPS/GPRS based digital geo fence. Each employee will be given a smart ID card with the ability for a two-way communication. The manager or central controlling authority gets mobile application to track the team on a real time basis. The SIM in the smart ID card ensures the tracking is accurate. Whenever the field force personnel venture out of the designed geo area, manager is alerted.

Key Features:

  1. Ccovers widely spread geo graphical area
  2. Precisely tracks people/assets outside premises
  3. Sends messages and two-way communication
  4. Sets up geo fencing based space rules per asset
  5. Sends alerts to mobiles, tablets or PCs on the internet
  6. Generates various tracking and security reports as per customer needs
