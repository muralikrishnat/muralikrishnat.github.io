GigaMesh IOT Platform

Decentralized Blockchain IOT Platform.

GigaMesh IOT is a blockchain based platform for the Internet of Things that allows designing and developing integrated IoT solutions, connected applications with smart contracts between people, organizations and IOT Devices. The objective of GigaMesh IOT platform is to provide a simple scalable, flexible, secure, interoperable system.

GigaMesh IOT smart contracts between organizations and individuals allow secure information transactions between devices owned by different organizations. Smart Contract defines bandwidth, privacy aspects, retention times of the data. As an example if Utility company is gathering information from an IOT device from a home, Owner of the home and Organization would agree on a smart contract of what, why, how, where about the information that will be gathered and transmitted to Organization. This information would be kept in a blockchain and will be there in information wallets of all parties involved in trasaction. IOT Device Owners can see what is being transmitted in visualizations and dashboards that will be built. Everyone involved in smart contract will be able to see the same information. Moreover if any organization try and transmit data further to any 3rdparties it will be recorded on the blockchain.

GigaMesh IOT platform makes integration with other "Non BlockChain" and "BlockChain" based IOT platforms simple and easy. GigaMesh IOT is going to be a hardware,software agnostic platform which inturn provides integration interfaces to be able to recieve information from connected devices, iot sensors, other IOT platforms.
